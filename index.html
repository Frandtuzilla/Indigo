Empowering Hispanic and Vulnerable Communities

Building Brighter Futures Through Education and Advocacy.

At Indigo Group, we believe that empowering vulnerable communities is key to creating a more equitable society. Our holistic programs in education, employment, legal support, and leadership development are designed to break down barriers and uplift Hispanic and immigrant communities in California.

Our Key Initiatives

WHAT WE DO

Education Programs

Providing ESL classes and civic engagement workshops to empower non-native English speakers.

Employment Support

Offering job training, resume building, and leadership development to improve employment opportunities.

Legal Assistance

Providing free legal support for immigrants and refugees, helping with asylum applications and family matters.

Leadership Development

Fostering leadership skills and financial literacy to help young Latinos take on leadership roles.
